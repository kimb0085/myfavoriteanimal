Pugs!

Pugs are the greastest companion animal for a number of reasons:

  1. Friendly with all dogs, cats, and humans alike
  2. Silence is never an option with a pug nearby: snorting, snoring, grunting, barking
  3. They love to give lots of kisses
  4. You'll never dine alone, or without a watchful, waiting eye
  5. Those smooshed faces and ample wrinkles are too cute
